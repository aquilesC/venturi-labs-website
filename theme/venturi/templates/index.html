The Missing Middle of Commercialization

The current deep tech commercialization model is fractured. Academic research produces highly valuable specialized tools, but they face a binary, often doomed, choice:

The Startup Trap

TTOs attempt to spin off a new company, requiring VC funding and a CEO search for specialized instruments that might only sell 20–50 units a year globally. The economics rarely work.

The Bench Trap

To avoid the friction of a spin-off, the tool lives only in a published paper, ultimately dying on the academic bench when the postdoc who built it graduates.
